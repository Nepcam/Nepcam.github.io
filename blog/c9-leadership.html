Do the Tonglen meditation. How did you find it?

Willingness is the keyword here in my opinion. In order to be happy you have to be compassionate. And to be compassionate you have to be willing to recieve the suffering and pain of others, and give relief, well-being, and peace in return. And then experience the ability to be transformers. But as I said in my opening sentence, you have to be willing to do this. It will not work if you are superficial, going for personal gains.
